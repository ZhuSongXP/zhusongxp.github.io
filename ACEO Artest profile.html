About

关于

I create ACEO (Art Cards Editions and Originals) — small-format artworks measuring 2.5 × 3.5 inches.

我创作 ACEO（Art Cards Editions and Originals）—— 尺寸为 2.5 × 3.5 英寸的小型艺术品。

My process begins with tea-stained or coffee-dyed paper, layered with fragments from vintage magazines, handwritten notes, and found objects. Each card is sealed with Mod Podge and assigned a unique digital identity.

我的创作从茶染或咖啡染的纸张开始，叠加复古杂志碎片、手写笔记与现成物。每张卡片以 Mod Podge 封层，并赋予独特的数字身份。

Every piece is part of a limited series. Scan the QR code on the back to view its full history, current owner, and provenance.

每件作品均为限量系列。扫描背面的二维码，查看完整历史、现任收藏者与流转记录。
